Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, company name, and message when you fill out our contact form or book a consultation. We do not collect sensitive personal data unless explicitly required for a service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to respond to your enquiries, provide our services, send relevant communications, and improve our website. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Google Authentication

Our Tools section offers an optional Google Sign-In feature. When you use this, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. This data is stored locally in your browser session only and is not transmitted to our servers.

4. Browser-Based Tools

All tools available on our Tools page run entirely in your browser. No files, images, or documents you process through these tools are uploaded to or stored on our servers.

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for website functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. Third-party services such as Google Fonts and Calendly may set their own cookies subject to their respective privacy policies.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website uses Google Fonts for typography, Calendly for appointment scheduling, and Google Identity Services for optional authentication. Each of these services operates under its own privacy policy.

7. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry. Browser session data (such as Google login state) is cleared when you log out or clear your browser storage.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at info.mysock@zohomail.in.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related queries, contact us at info.mysock@zohomail.in or WhatsApp us at +91 8057470837.
