About MySock

Who We Are

A Startup Built to Solve a Real Problem

MySock is an AI-driven digital solutions company founded with one clear goal: help offline and small businesses build a powerful online presence — without the complexity, cost, or confusion that usually comes with it.

We're a lean, focused team of developers, designers, and digital strategists who understand what it takes to grow a business from the ground up. We've worked with coaching institutes, retail shops, educational institutions, and startups — and we bring the same energy and commitment to every project.

We're not a generic agency. We're a growth partner — hands-on, results-driven, and invested in your success.
